Ian brought 45 mph gusts, more than 2 inches of rain to Forsyth

Oct 02, 2022  | 
By John Deem
 |  Winston-Salem Journal Verified

The most-severe conditions locally were between 6 p.m. and 9 p.m. Friday, when sustained winds of 25-30 mph and gusts in the 40-45 mph range were recorded at Smith Reynolds Airport. Gusts of at least 40 mph were reported for four consecutive hours.
