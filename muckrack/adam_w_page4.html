Jul 22, 2022  | 
By Adam Wagner Verified
 |  News & Observer Verified
A sweeping piece of legislation introduced by powerful U.S. Senate Democrats would lift a Trump-era moratorium on new offshore wind leases in the Atlantic Ocean off the coasts of North Carolina and other Southeastern states. The 10-year moratorium went into effect July 1 despite efforts to omit offshore wind led by wind industry trade groups and a bipartisan group of congressmen including North Carolina’s Deborah Ross and David Rouzer.
