Winston-Salem limits use of pine straw for landscaping

Dec 10, 2021  | 
By John Deem
 |  Winston-Salem Journal Verified
A Southern landscaping staple will no longer be an option in areas close to many commercial buildings and apartments in Winston-Salem. An ordinance approved by the Winston-Salem City Council in November will prohibit so-called pine straw or other highly combustible material from being used as groundcover within 10 feet of businesses and multi-family residential buildings with flammable exteriors. The ordinance applies to any groundcover that can burn at a rate of two feet per minute.
