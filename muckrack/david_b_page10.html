By David Boraks Verified
 |  Blue Ridge Public Radio Verified

Two years ago, the city contracted a company called Ecoplexus to build the project, which would supply solar power through Duke Energy. But Ecoplexus now says it needs a 25% cost increase or it will default on the project.
